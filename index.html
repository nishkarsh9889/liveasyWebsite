Who are we?

Pranav Gupta founder of Liveasy belongs to a trucking background, his father told him about what his father and his customers were going through, from here he got an idea of building an app. Then he did his research and found out that the problem is big, the market is huge and untapped. He believes this is a great opportunity to serve people.

Our mission

The digital revolution has been a part of almost all sectors and industries worldwide. Characterized as a union of technologies that is blurring the lines between the physical, digital, and biological spheres, this technological innovation is boosting productivity, opening newer markets and driving economic growth.As the technology is advancing, Liveasy wants to digitize trucking industry. Trucking is the backbone of every economy around the world but not in India, this is a chaotic platform and there is not much technology involved. People are frequently connected on phone calls but they are not able to connect with the truckers efficiently.

Top new Innovations in the Trucking Industry has led to a significant shift of power from the seller to the consumer. Our main goal is to organize this industry using technology. We aim to empower the small-medium businesses so that they are able to generate higher profits, as well are able to operate proficiently by using technology. Technology has made it much easier for us to assess what consumers want, and so we can provide them with a steady supply. Moreover, trucking fleet maintenance has also become much easier and less time-consuming with the introduction of more advanced trucks. As customers are unenlightened of how much technology has changed the trucking industry, they are following the traditional way to get services done. Let’s certify SMEs using technology and help them widen their buisness.
