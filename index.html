About Us

CargoPro is TMS provider that delivers innovative freight and logistics management platforms that automate, track, and optimize end-to-end supply chain operations for businesses. Our comprehensive solutions tackle common business pain points like freight sourcing, indent management, complex finances, paper invoicing, and outdated tracking. CargoPro dashboard integrate planning, execution, settlement, and analysis to boost efficiency, visibility, and cost savings across transportation sourcing.

Automated Solutions for Your Logistics Operations

All in One Dashboard

Gain full control of your supply chain with CargoPro's smart dashboard. Access real-time data and automation tools for streamlined logistics management.

Analytics

Access vital logistics insights with our analytics feature. Monitor placement efficiency, ratings, loading/unloading times, halt durations, transit details, and turnaround for optimized operations.

Live Tracking

Enable transparent vehicle operations with SIM-based tracking. Monitor real-time vehicle locations, routes, stops, and status using integrated SIM cards and GPS technology.

Freight Sourcing and Reverse Auction

Indent Automation

In Plant Movement and Optimization
