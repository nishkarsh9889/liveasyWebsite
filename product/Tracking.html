Tracking

Our tracking solutions offer real-time visibility into shipments, vehicles, drivers, and field workforce, ensuring on-time deliveries, proactive communication, and superior customer experience. With advanced tracking capabilities, elevate your logistics operations to the next level.

How Does it Work?

SIM-Based Tracking for Full Transperency

Implement SIM-based tracking to ensure full transparency and visibility into vehicle operations. By utilizing SIM cards integrated with GPS tracking technology, you can monitor vehicle locations and movements in real-time. This approach provides accurate data on routes taken, stops made, and overall vehicle status.

Full Route History

Implement a robust tracking system to monitor every checkpoint along with the total duration spent at each checkpoint. This system captures detailed information about the vehicle's journey, including entry and exit times at specific checkpoints. By recording timestamps at each checkpoint, the system calculates the duration spent at each location, providing insights into the overall transit time and operational efficiency.

Play Full Trip

Access a playback option to review the entire trip by selecting specific dates and timestamps. This feature allows you to visualize the vehicle's journey within the chosen timeframe, observing checkpoints and activities along the route. This streamlined functionality supports efficient monitoring and analysis of travel patterns and trip performance.
